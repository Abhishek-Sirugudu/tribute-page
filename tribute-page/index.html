Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

The 11th President of India, widely known as the "People's President".

Key Contributions:

  • Developed the Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III).
  • Served as the Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister.
  • Pivotal role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Authored several inspirational books like "Wings of Fire".
